## 🚀 Why Our Method Works
Our curriculum is designed using the **Hub-and-Spoke** model to ensure you don't just memorize formulas, but understand the "why" behind them.

### 1. Identify the Pattern
Every algebra problem follows a pattern. We teach you how to spot the "Greatest Common Factor" or the "Difference of Squares" before you even pick up a pencil.

### 2. Avoid the Traps
We highlight the **5 Common Mistakes** for every major topic so you can catch errors before they happen on your exam.

### 3. Practice with Purpose
Use our interactive sliders and Q&A tools to test your knowledge in real-time.
