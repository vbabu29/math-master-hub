Factoring polynomials is one of the most important Algebra I skills because it connects expressions, equations, and graphing. This hub groups the lesson content and the focused practice work in one place.

In this hub, you will:

- Review the most common factoring mistakes students make
- Practice factoring out GCFs and factoring trinomials
- Recognize and factor difference of squares expressions

## Explore This Hub

- Start with **5 Common Mistakes When Factoring Trinomials** for explanations and common pitfalls
- Open **Practice Questions** to work through the factoring sequence and a mixed miscellaneous set
