Factoring trinomials is one of the most important Algebra I skills because it connects expressions, equations, and graphing. This hub groups the lesson content and the practice work in one place.

In this hub, you will:

- Review the most common factoring mistakes students make
- Practice simple trinomials and coefficient-based factoring
- Build confidence checking your answers with FOIL

## Explore This Hub

- Start with **5 Common Mistakes When Factoring Trinomials** for explanations and common pitfalls
- Open **Practice Questions** to test what you learned
